How to Play

Wordy Master is a fully cooperative word-based guessing game in which the Guide must help the Seekers find a secret word from multiple hint provided

  • Guess the correct five letter word
  •   Green Signifies a correct letter in the right spot
  •   Orange Signifies a correct letter in a wrong spot
  •   Ash Signifies a letter not present in the correct word
